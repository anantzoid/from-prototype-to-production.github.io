Dr. Jochen Papenbrock

Head of Financial Technology - EMEA, NVIDIA
Dr. Jochen is head of financial technology EMEA / lead developer relations manager banking global at NVIDIA, the globally leading accelerated computing platform company. He has spent the last 25 years in various roles on the topic of AI in financial services. He works with executives, data scientists, developers, and partners in the global NVIDIA ecosystem. Jochen is a financial data scientist and received his degree and Ph.D. from the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT). Before NVIDIA he worked as a consultant, entrepreneur, and researcher with asset managers, banks, insurance companies, and central banks.

Dr. Subhabrata Das

Dr. Subhabrata Das is a distinguished data scientist with a strong background in computational physics. He holds a Ph.D. in Engineering Physics from Columbia University and a Master's degree from Cornell University. Currently serving as the Lead Data Scientist at JPMorgan Chase, Dr. Das focuses on optimizing client onboarding processes through data-driven solutions. With a diverse career spanning across tech giants and financial institutions, Dr. Das has held key positions at Microsoft, Ally Financial, etc. His expertise encompasses a wide range of data science applications, including outage prediction, fraud detection, language translation automation, and Monte Carlo simulations. As a thought leader in data science, Dr. Das regularly contributes to industry conferences and academic journals, advancing the field of analytics and decision-making in the financial sector.
